ShopPic Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

ShopPic is a utility app that helps you quickly search for products across shopping platforms. We take your privacy seriously.

Data Collection

ShopPic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. All processing happens entirely on your device:

  • Images you share are saved locally in the app's private storage
  • Search history is stored only on your device using standard iOS local storage
  • No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs

Data Sharing

ShopPic does not share any data with third parties. When you tap a platform button, the image is copied to your system clipboard so you can paste it into the shopping app. Clipboard data never leaves your device and is never accessed by us.

Internet Connectivity

ShopPic does not make any network requests. It works completely offline.

Third-Party Apps

When you tap a platform button (Taobao, JD, etc.), iOS opens that app if installed. ShopPic has no control over and is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party apps.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
